About the Game

Aeon Must Die! is a dynamic beat 'em-up developped by Limestone Games and published by Focus Entertainment. The game features breakneck risk-reward gameplay and tactical fighting, all wrapped in a unique anime-inspired art style. Players embody Aeon, the betrayed Emperor of the Void Armada, navigating the futuristic city of Pantheon to exact revenge while battling for control over their own body.
